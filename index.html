Welcome to WIUT Intranet!

Westminster International University in Tashkent (WIUT) is the first international university located in the heart of Central Asia to offer a Western-style education with UK qualifications. WIUT offers a wide range of programs, academic and research activities, as well as social events. Independent learning is an essential part of the UK education system, and WIUT is no exception.
